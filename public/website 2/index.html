Project 360

Project 360 is a one-of-a-kind initiative by us at Edunext. It aims to eliminate the vital problems that arise during the remote learning procedures that are currently in place to aid in the overall development of a student both academically and socially. Apart from state-of-the-art facilities in academics, a plethora of counsellors and motivators are always ready to assist your child to cross all hurdles in life.
